parts you will need to buy:
2x small drone corelles motors
1x LiPo 500mah battery
Rc-controller and reciever (me i used one i found at home in a dead Rc-car)
1x tennis grip or any other simmilar thing for the top cover that water doesn't pass trough

step by step guide:
1.print the parts (no supports needed for the hull and cover) supports needed for proppeler(reccomended to print with 0.2mm nozzle
2.put the 2 motors inside the holes (it's a little complicated so you might want to fix the hull file)
3.wire the motors wires on the reciever pcb you found/buyed/created
4.then after you had done all the previous steps put a two sided tape on the top cover or glue to put the tennis grip
